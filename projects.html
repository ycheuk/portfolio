Reed Senate Website
Website Developer / Designer

The Reed Senate website offers essential functions for the student body at Reed College such as funding polls, student body elections, and improved access to student club events. This user-friendly website, created from scratch using HTML, CSS and Javascript, simplifies the process of securing funds and conducting elections, enhancing the overall student experience. Continuously evolving through maintenance and development, it adapts to meet changing student needs, making it a dynamic hub for fostering campus engagement and participation.
