Projects

Reed Senate Website
Website Developer / Designer

The Reed Senate website offers essential functions for the student body at Reed College such as funding polls, student body elections, and improved access to student club events. This user-friendly website, created from scratch using HTML, CSS and Javascript, simplifies the process of securing funds and conducting elections, enhancing the overall student experience. Continuously evolving through maintenance and development, it adapts to meet changing student needs, making it a dynamic hub for fostering campus engagement and participation.

ASCII Chronicles: A Day in the Digital Universe
Website Developer / Designer

Embark on a mesmerizing journey through ASCII Chronicles: A Day in the Digital Universe, a web experience blending art and interactivity. Coded using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this game invites you to freely navigate an open world rendered entirely in ASCII art. It offers a captivating exploration where every pixel tells a story, creating a delightful synergy between the simplicity of ASCII characters and the intricacies of web development. Immerse yourself in this whimsical landscape, where the art of code meets the beauty of ASCII in a truly distinctive online adventure.

Skribbl.io
Website Developer / Designer

Skribbl.io is a digital rendition of the classic game of pictionary, where players utilize a chatroom to guess answers and control gameplay. This project was heavily inspired by the popular online game, skribbl.io.
Skribbl.io was made by Emily Yu and Yik Yin Cheuk as part of our 2024 Hackathon. This Hackathon acted as an introduction to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, web development, client-server interactions, etc., and Skribbl.io was the capstone of our week-long learning experience!
This game was created with HTML/CSS/JavaScript, with the help of runtime environments/frameworks/etc. such as Node.js, Express.js, and Socket.IO for our backend.

Brother's Buffet Website is the go-to place to explore all that the Chinese buffet restaurant has to offer. Paideia
Event & Graphic Designer

Paideia, one of the biggest annual events on the Reed College campus, gives students the chance to take over a classroom and plunge into a subject they are passionate about. Castle Suspense
Game Developer / Designer

Castle Suspense is a thrilling murder mystery game that puts you in the shoes of one of six suspects trying to solve a murder case. As a player, you will explore a mysterious castle, gather clues, and solve puzzles to uncover the truth behind the Old Duke's death. The catch? You are suffering from amnesia and cannot remember your own past, making the investigation even more challenging. With its engaging storyline, Python3 coding, and visually stunning ASCII art, Castle Suspense provides an exciting gaming experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end. Can you find the murderer and escape the castle alive?

Social Change Leadership Program
Event & Graphic Designer

The Social Change Leadership Program (SCLP) is a transformative program that develops leadership skills and fosters community engagement through the Social Change Model of Leadership. As a SCLP Beta-Builder, I had the privilege to contribute to the program's development, design, and review of the SCLP curriculum. The program aims to empower students who have not had the opportunity to get involved with the community and connect them with local companies to create positive social change. By participating in the SCLP, students will enhance their leadership skills and gain the knowledge and experience to make a difference in their community.
